About Unique Cookies

Unique Cookies was born in my family kitchen in Brazil 34 years ago. A seven year old girl baking with her grandmother's 70 years old mixer and a dream to share her delicious cookies with the world.

Our story

What started as a simple way to raise money at church became a lifelong love for desserts, hospitality and making people feel cared for.

Years later that same love, faith and passion became Unique Cookies, a company built on God, excellence and connection. Every box is a little piece of that story, sent from my kitchen to your home.

Why our cookies are different

We use coconut oil, noisette, ghee and luxury chocolate to create a rich, indulgent experience that feels like it came from a luxury bakery, without all the work in your kitchen.

Our cookies are already baked for you. Store them in the freezer for up to 3 months, warm them for a few seconds and enjoy gourmet cookies anytime you crave.

Our mission

Our mission is to create unforgettable moments of joy with cookies that taste homemade, feel luxurious and are always ready anytime you crave. Just open the box, warm them up and enjoy pure cookie joy.

From family nights and movie nights to office celebrations and gifts for clients, Unique Cookies is here to make your life easier and sweeter.
